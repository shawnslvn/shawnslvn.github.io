About Me

I graduated from the University of Indianapolis in 2022 with a Bachelor's Degree in Business Management and Administration. While my career began in construction sales, I quickly discovered a passion for data analysis and the power of data-driven decision-making.

Working in sales has given me firsthand experience with market trends, customer behavior, and business operations—insights that sparked my curiosity about how data can optimize performance and drive strategic growth. Since then, I’ve been honing my skills in SQL, Python, and data visualization tools, working on projects that showcase my ability to transform raw data into actionable insights.

As I continue my transition into the data analytics field, I’m eager to learn, grow, and connect with professionals who share my enthusiasm for data. Let’s connect and explore how data can make a difference!
